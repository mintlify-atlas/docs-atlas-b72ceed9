## What is a Requester?

In Servineo, a **Requester** is a user who needs services and hires fixers (service providers) to complete jobs. Requesters can browse available service offers, create job requests, schedule appointments, and manage payments.

## Key Features

### Profile Management

Requesters can manage their profile information including:

- **Phone Number**: Contact information for communication with fixers
- **Location**: Default location for service requests
- **Preferences**: Service categories and notification settings

### Location-Based Services

The platform uses geolocation to:

- Find nearby fixers
- Display service offers in your area
- Set job request locations on an interactive map
- Track appointment locations

<Note>
You can update your location at any time through the profile edit page. The system uses OpenStreetMap for geocoding and reverse geocoding.
</Note>

### Search and Discovery

Requesters have access to powerful search and filtering capabilities:

- **Text Search**: Search by keywords, service names, or fixer names
- **Filters**: Filter by city, category, price range, and ratings
- **Sorting**: Sort results by relevance, price, rating, or date
- **View Modes**: Switch between list, grid, and map views

### Job Request Workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse Offers">
    Search and filter available service offers from fixers
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Request">
    Submit a job request with description, location, and time preferences
  </Step>
  <Step title="Schedule Appointment">
    Once accepted, schedule an appointment with the fixer
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete & Pay">
    After service completion, make payment and leave a review
  </Step>
</Steps>

## User Interface

The requester interface is designed for ease of use:

- **Responsive Design**: Works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
- **Multilingual Support**: Available in multiple languages using next-intl
- **Real-time Updates**: Live status updates for appointments and payments
- **Interactive Maps**: Leaflet-based maps for location selection

<Info>
All requester actions are logged for analytics and to improve the platform experience. This includes searches, clicks on job offers, and filter usage.
</Info>

## Getting Started

To start using Servineo as a requester:

1. **Complete Your Profile**: Add your phone number and default location
2. **Browse Services**: Explore available job offers in your area
3. **Create a Request**: Submit your first job request
4. **Schedule an Appointment**: Book a time with your chosen fixer
5. **Make Payment**: Use QR code or other payment methods

<Warning>
Always verify fixer ratings and reviews before creating a job request. Check the fixer's profile for service history and customer feedback.
</Warning>
