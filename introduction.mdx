## What is Servineo?

Servineo is a comprehensive service marketplace platform that bridges the gap between customers seeking services (Requesters) and skilled service providers (Fixers). Whether you need plumbing, electrical work, carpentry, cleaning, or any other professional service, Servineo makes it easy to find, book, and pay for quality services.

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an Account">
    Sign up as a Requester to find services or as a Fixer to offer your skills. Use Google OAuth or email registration.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search & Connect">
    Requesters search for services and browse fixer profiles. Fixers receive job offers matching their skills.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Schedule & Communicate">
    Book appointments through the integrated calendar. Communicate details and confirm service requirements.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete & Pay">
    Service is delivered and payment is processed securely through Stripe. Rate and review the experience.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Platform Benefits

### For Requesters

- **Verified Providers**: Browse profiles with ratings, reviews, and work history
- **Flexible Scheduling**: Book appointments at times that work for you
- **Transparent Pricing**: See rates upfront and compare multiple providers
- **Secure Payments**: Multiple payment methods including credit card, QR code, and cash

### For Fixers

- **Business Growth**: Access a steady stream of potential customers
- **Digital Wallet**: Manage earnings and track payment history
- **Professional Profile**: Showcase your skills, experience, and portfolio
- **Flexible Work**: Choose which jobs to accept based on your availability

## Technology Stack

Servineo is built with modern web technologies:

- **Frontend**: Next.js 15, React 19, TypeScript
- **State Management**: Redux Toolkit with RTK Query
- **UI Components**: Material-UI, Radix UI, Tailwind CSS
- **Integrations**: Stripe, Google OAuth, Leaflet Maps, reCAPTCHA
